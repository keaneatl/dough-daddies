Daddy's got you covered.

We are Dough Daddies, and we are a Filipino-Italian fusion bakery & restaurant. We firmly believe that dough is the foundation of a multitude of dishes that give amazing taste and satisfaction. The company accentuates a rustic theme carried by class, creating an ecosystem and environment of a home and elegance. Dough daddies serves as the cradle of crafting food that makes you feel at home but also provide a sense of class, regardless of their race, religion, color, creed, and sexual orientation. We firmly believe in breaking barriers and pushing for an environment where our food and service radiates acceptance.
